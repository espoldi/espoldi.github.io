About Me

My name is Emily Spoldi. I am 28 years old and programming has had a heavy influence on my life since I was little. I was the only one in my immediate family who decided to do something other than programming when deciding on college majors... UNTIL NOW!

Having grown up in an era where technology was evolving quickly has given me the ability to use some systems without much experience.
